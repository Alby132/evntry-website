How the fee works

On the free Doors plan, Evntry takes 5% of each ticket you sell. On Headline and Tour it's 0%. Fans always pay exactly what you set — no booking fee added on their end.

When am I billed?

Monthly plans are billed on the same date each month. Per-ticket fees are deducted at the time of each sale via Stripe. Cancel any time.

What about Stripe fees?

Stripe charges ~1.4% + €0.25 per European card transaction. That's separate from Evntry's fee and standard across all ticketing platforms.

Questions

The obvious
answers.

Do fans pay any extra fees?
No. The price you set is exactly what fans pay at checkout. Evntry never adds booking fees on the fan side. That's one of the reasons fans actually complete the purchase — no sticker shock at the end.
Do I own my attendee data?
Yes, completely. Your attendee list — names, emails, purchase history — belongs to you. You can export it anytime. We never share it with third parties or use it for our own marketing. Unlike some platforms, we don't hold your data hostage when you leave.
How do I get paid?
Directly through Stripe. When a fan buys a ticket, the revenue goes to your connected Stripe account — minus the Evntry fee (5% on Doors, 0% on Headline) and Stripe's standard card processing fee (~1.4% + €0.25 for European cards). Payouts follow Stripe's normal schedule — typically 2–7 business days.
Can I sell free tickets?
Yes. Free events have no fees at all — no 5%, nothing. You can also mix free and paid tiers on the same event (e.g. free general entry, paid VIP table). Free ticket tiers are always free regardless of your plan.
What if I only do a few events a year?
Stay on Doors. It's completely free — you only pay the 5% when tickets actually sell. No monthly cost, no risk. Upgrade when volume makes the math work in your favour.
When does Headline pay off?
Headline costs €49/month with 0% per ticket, vs Doors which is free but takes 5%. The break-even is €980/month in ticket revenue — roughly 1–2 decent events. Sell more than that and Headline is the cheaper plan.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel before your next billing date and you won't be charged again. Your events and attendee data stay accessible.
Is there a Stripe fee on top of the Evntry fee?
Yes — Stripe charges their own payment processing fee separately (typically ~1.4% + €0.25 for European cards, higher for non-EU cards). This goes directly to Stripe, not to us. On Headline, the only fee is Stripe's — Evntry takes nothing per ticket.
Is Evntry available in my country?
Evntry is rolling out across Europe. Stripe payments work in all major European markets. If you're outside the EU, email us at hello@evntry.app and we'll let you know the timeline.
