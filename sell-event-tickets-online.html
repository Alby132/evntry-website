How it works

Three steps to
your first sale.

No onboarding call. No training session. No support ticket. Just create an account and go.

Step 1

Create your event

Add a name, date, ticket price, and capacity. Connect your Stripe account to receive payments. Your event is ready in under 5 minutes — seriously.

Step 2

Share your link

You get a dedicated ticket page at a clean URL. Share it anywhere — Instagram bio, Facebook event, WhatsApp, your website. Fans buy in seconds without creating an account.

Step 3

Scan at the door

Open Evntry on your phone and scan QR codes as guests arrive. Know exactly who's checked in, who's still outstanding, and how many tickets are left — live, on your screen.
