Our mission

Events belong to the people
who make them.

We built Evntry because great music nights deserve better tools — and the people running them deserve to keep more of what they earn.

The problem

Most ticketing platforms are built for the platform, not the organizer. They take 10–15% per ticket, lock you out of your own attendee data, and make you contact support just to update an event.

Independent venue owners and event organizers — the people actually making culture happen — get the worst deal.

Why we built this

So we built Evntry — simple ticketing for people who run nights. Set up an event in minutes, sell tickets with no fees for your fans, and scan the door from your phone.

You keep your revenue. You own your attendee list. No lock-in, no surprise charges, no begging for support.

What we believe

Built on three ideas

🎟

Built for independence

No platform lock-in, no percentage skimmed off the top forever. Your event, your money, your audience relationship.

🤝

Honest pricing

Free tier with a small transaction fee when you're starting out. Flat monthly plans when you're ready to grow. Nothing hidden.

🔒

Your data, always

Attendee lists, ticket sales, analytics — they belong to you. Always. We're a tool, not a gatekeeper.
