Privacy
Policy

Last updated: 22 May 2026

This policy explains what personal data Evntry collects, why, and what rights you have over it. We've written it to be readable, not just legally compliant.

Who we are

Data controller: Evntry (Alberto Surina)
Contact: hello@evntry.app
Website: evntry.app

For any privacy-related questions, email us directly at hello@evntry.app. We respond within 48 hours.

What data we collect

On the marketing site (evntry.app), we collect:

  • Email address — when you submit an early access request form. This is the only data we collect on this site.

On the app (app.evntry.app), we collect:

  • Account information — name, email address, password (hashed), and role (venue owner, musician, or fan)
  • Event data — event details, ticket tiers, and attendance information you create
  • Ticket purchase data — buyer name, email, and payment confirmation (processed by Stripe)
  • Profile information — venue or musician profile details you choose to add

Why we collect it

  • Early access emails — to contact you about access to Evntry (legal basis: consent)
  • Account data — to provide the service, authenticate you, and operate your events (legal basis: contract performance)
  • Ticket data — to issue tickets, send confirmations, and enable door scanning (legal basis: contract performance)
  • Communications — to send you transactional emails (ticket confirmations, event updates) you have requested (legal basis: contract performance)

Who we share data with

We do not sell your data. We share it only with the services needed to operate Evntry:

  • Formspree — processes early access form submissions (email only)
  • Stripe — handles payment processing for ticket purchases. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Resend — sends transactional emails (ticket confirmations, event blasts)
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this marketing website
  • Cloudflare — provides DNS, CDN, and basic analytics (no cookies, no personal data)

Cookies

The marketing site (evntry.app) uses no cookies. Cloudflare Analytics, which we use for traffic data, does not set cookies or collect personal data.

The app (app.evntry.app) uses a session cookie to keep you logged in. This is strictly necessary for the service to function.

How long we keep your data

  • Early access emails — until you ask to be removed, or 12 months from collection if we never contacted you
  • Account data — for as long as your account is active, plus 30 days after deletion
  • Ticket data — 3 years (required for financial record-keeping)

Your rights under GDPR

If you're in the EU or EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@evntry.app. We'll respond within 30 days.

Supervisory authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Croatian data protection authority:

AZOP — Agencija za zaštitu osobnih podataka
Website: azop.hr
Email: azop@azop.hr

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. For significant changes that affect your rights, we'll notify you by email.

Questions? Email hello@evntry.app — we're a small team and we reply personally.
