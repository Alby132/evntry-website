The problem

Every event.
The same
three problems.

Before the night, during it, and after. Evntry fixes all three.

Before the event

You never know who's actually coming

Paper lists, WhatsApp reservations, inbox messages — spread across three places. Someone says they're coming. Do they mean it? You find out at the door. A paid ticket is a real commitment. An "interested" click isn't.

On the night

The door is always chaos

Cash box, handwritten list, someone's phone with a spreadsheet. The first 30 minutes of every event is a queue and an argument. Slow entry kills the vibe before the first song even starts.

After the event

You have nothing to show for it

You had a great night. 150 people loved it. Then it's over — no names, no emails, no data. Next week you're back to zero, promoting to the void, hoping the same crowd sees your post.

Questions

Common
questions.

Do fans pay extra fees?
No. The price you set is exactly what fans pay at checkout. Evntry never adds booking fees on the fan side — no sticker shock, no drop-off at the last step.
How quickly do I get paid?
Via your connected Stripe account. Payouts follow Stripe's schedule — typically 2–7 business days after each sale. No waiting for a monthly settlement from a platform.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No contract, no lock-in. Cancel anytime. The Doors plan is free forever — you only move to Headline if you decide it makes sense for your volume.
Do I own my attendee data?
Completely. Your attendee list — names, emails, purchase history — belongs to you and is exportable anytime. We never share it or use it for our own marketing.
Do I need a credit card to get started?
No. The Doors plan is free with no card required. You only need a Stripe account to receive payments from ticket sales — Stripe's setup is free and takes about 10 minutes.
