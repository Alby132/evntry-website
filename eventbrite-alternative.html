Why venues switch

Three reasons venues
leave Eventbrite.

These aren't edge cases. Every venue owner we talk to brings up the same things.

The fees

Fans pay more than your ticket price

Eventbrite adds a 10–15% service charge on top of whatever price you set. Fans see it at checkout, feel cheated, and sometimes abandon the purchase. Your reputation takes the hit — not theirs.

The data

Your attendees aren't really yours

Every fan who buys through Eventbrite becomes an Eventbrite user first. Your access to their emails and data is at the platform's discretion. When you leave, you leave empty-handed.

The complexity

Built for festivals, not your jazz night

Eventbrite is designed for large-scale events. Most of its features are noise for a bar or club. Thirty minutes to set up a single ticket page shouldn't be normal — and with Evntry, it isn't.
